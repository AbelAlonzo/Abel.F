Beard & Face

Take care of your beard and face with the best tips and products for a flawless look

Beard Styles

Short Beard

Perfect for a professional look and easy to maintain. Ideal for office.

  • Trim every 2-3 days
  • Use beard oil
  • Define the lines
  • Maintain symmetry

Medium Beard

Perfect balance between style and comfort. Versatile and attractive.

  • Trim weekly
  • Use styling wax
  • Brush regularly
  • Hydrate with oils

Long Beard

For the boldest. Requires more care but the result is spectacular.

  • Patience to grow
  • Intensive care
  • Specialized products
  • Professional trimming

Facial Care

Cleansing

Clean your face twice a day with a gentle cleanser to remove impurities.

Hydration

Apply a moisturizer suitable for your skin type to keep it soft.

Sunscreen

Use sunscreen daily to protect your skin from UV rays.

Exfoliation

Exfoliate your face 1-2 times a week to remove dead cells.
